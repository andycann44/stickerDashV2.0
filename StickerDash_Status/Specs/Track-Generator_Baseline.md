
## Natural Language Prompt (tiny)
- Text area + **Apply NL** button in Track Lab.
- Supported: 
  - `create <L> m by <W> m`
  - `straighten rows A-B`
  - `offset rows A-B x <m>`
  - `offset rows A-B y <m>`
  - `append straight <L> m [step <s>]`
  - `delete row N`, `delete rows A-B`
- Anything else logs a warning; no other changes.
